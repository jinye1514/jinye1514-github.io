about me

Hello! I'm a graduate student in the Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering - Research (MSME-R) program at Carnegie Mellon University (CMU). My academic focus is on robotics, and I'm particularly interested in robot planning and control. At CMU, I've taken courses on manipulator kinematics and dynamics, state space control, optimal control, and robot learning. Over the past year, I have been doing research at the Biorobotics Lab at the Robotics Institute, and I have had hands-on experience with formulating and implementing control algorithms for legged robots and high-DOF robotic arms in simulation and on real hardware. I am currently looking for internship or full-time opportunities in robotics starting in Summer 2025. If you also love robots and would like to chat, please feel free to reach out to me at my email!

Outside of robotics, I enjoy working out, climbing, skiing, hiking, and other outdoor activities.
